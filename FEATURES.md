# Lowkey - Feature Overview

**Lowkey** is a curated video platform for product launch videos and announcements — essentially "Mobbin for launch videos." It's a gallery where users can browse, search, and watch the best product launch announcements from top companies for inspiration.

---

## Current Features

### Core Experience
- **Video Grid** — Responsive grid (2-6 columns) with search and style filters (Kinetic, 3D, Motion, Demo)
- **Video Details Page** — Full video player with metadata, credits, and related videos
- **Advanced Modal Player** — HLS streaming, quality/speed selectors, volume control, fullscreen, segmented seek bar

### Content Organization
- **Chapters** — Clickable chapter timeline for navigation
- **Segments** — Video breakdown (intro, problem, solution, in-action, CTA)
- **Transcripts** — Full transcripts with timestamps
- **Comments** — User comments section

### Authentication & Access
- **Freemium Model** — 12 free videos, rest locked behind auth
- **Auth Modal** — Login/signup flow with email verification
- **Locked Previews** — Grayscale cards with "Sign Up to Unlock" CTA

### Submission & Discovery
- **3-Step Submission Form** — Users can submit videos with validation
- **Related Videos** — Recommendations based on style/type scoring
- **Filter Sidebar** — Filter by video style with count badges

### Polish
- **Progressive Blur Navbar** — 6-layer blur gradient effect
- **Grid Size Persistence** — localStorage remembers preference
- **Design System Library** — Showcase page for components

## Feature Ideas

### Engagement & Social
1. **Collections/Playlists** — Let users save favorites and create themed collections ("Best SaaS launches", "Mobile app inspiration")
2. **Upvoting/Reactions** — Let users vote on videos, surface trending content
3. **Share with Timestamp** — Share links that start at a specific chapter/moment
4. **Follow Companies** — Get notified when a new video from a company you follow is added

### Discovery & Search
5. **Advanced Filters** — Filter by product type, duration range, year, company size
6. **AI-Powered Tags** — Auto-tag videos with techniques used (split screen, typography animation, 3D product shots)
7. **Similar Videos** — "More like this" based on visual style analysis
8. **Weekly Digest** — Email with top new additions

### Learning & Insights
9. **Breakdown Mode** — Side-by-side view showing techniques used at each moment
10. **Stats Dashboard** — Trends in launch video styles over time
11. **Copy Analysis** — Highlight key messaging patterns and CTAs
12. **Duration Insights** — "Average launch video is 90 seconds" type stats

### Creator Tools
13. **Moodboard Generator** — Select clips/frames to export as a moodboard PDF
14. **Frame Capture** — Screenshot any frame with one click
15. **Notes/Annotations** — Personal notes on videos for reference
16. **Compare View** — Side-by-side comparison of two videos

### Premium Features
17. **Download Transcripts** — Export as SRT or text
18. **API Access** — For agencies to integrate into their workflow
19. **Team Workspaces** — Share collections with your team
20. **Brand Pages** — Dedicated pages for companies showing all their launches

### Polish & UX
21. **Keyboard Shortcuts** — J/K to navigate, L to like, S to save
22. **Theater Mode** — Dimmed background, larger player
23. **Autoplay Queue** — Watch related videos automatically
24. **Mobile App** — Native experience with offline downloads
